About the Product

What is Post Day One Step? {P}

Post Day One Step is an FDA-approved emergency contraceptive pill designed to prevent pregnancy after unprotected sex or contraceptive failure. It works best when taken within 72 hours of intercourse.

What does it contain? {P}

Post Day One Step contains Levonorgestrel 1.5 mg, a progestin hormone that has been used in contraceptives for decades. It is available over the counter without a prescription.

What is the MSRP? {P}

The Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) for Post Day One Step is $24.99. Prices may vary slightly depending on the retailer or if purchasing online with added shipping options.

Do I need a prescription? {P}

No. Post Day One Step is available over the counter without a prescription at most pharmacies and can also be purchased online directly from our site.

How It Works

How does Post Day One Step work? {P}

Post Day One Step works by delaying ovulation, preventing the release of an egg from the ovaries, which reduces the chances of pregnancy. It does not terminate an existing pregnancy.

How effective is it? {P}

When taken within 72 hours, Post Day One Step is up to 89% effective in preventing pregnancy. The sooner you take it, the more effective it is.

How long after unprotected sex can I take it? {P}

Post Day One Step is most effective when taken within 72 hours (3 days) of unprotected sex, but it can be taken up to 5 days in some cases, though efficacy decreases over time.

Can I take it if I'm already pregnant? {P}

No. Post Day One Step will not terminate an existing pregnancy. It works by preventing pregnancy before it starts.

Safety & Side Effects

Are there any side effects? {P}

Some users may experience mild side effects such as nausea, fatigue, dizziness, or headaches. These typically go away within 24 hours. If symptoms persist, consult a healthcare provider.

Will it affect my future fertility? {P}

No. Taking Post Day One Step will not affect your future fertility or ability to conceive.

Can I take it more than once? {P}

Yes, Post Day One Step can be used more than once. However, it is not intended for regular use as a primary form of contraception. If you need ongoing contraception, speak with your healthcare provider about options.

Is it safe for most women? {P}

Post Day One Step is safe for most women. However, if you have a history of liver disease, unexplained vaginal bleeding, or are taking certain medications, consult your healthcare provider before use.
